Average Happiness Score of Three Groups

In this project, we divide total 150 countries into three fifity-member groups:

  • High Happiness Group containing top 50 highest happines score countries
  • Middle Happiness with 51 to 100 highest happiness score countries
  • Low Happiness with bottom 50 happiness score countries

This bar chart demonstrate the average happiness score in each group. Annotation will demonstrate the countries with highest happiness score and lowest happiness score. Hovering your mouse over the bar and wait for a few seconds will show you some representative countries in each group.
