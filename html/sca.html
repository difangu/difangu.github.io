Scatter Plot: Happiness Score vs. Factors

In this section, we are intetesd in behavior between happiness score and the rest of factors (i.e. GDP per capita, social support, healthy life expectancy, freedom to make life choices, generosity, and perceptions of corruption.) By click on the dropmenu located on the top-left corner, users can select the second variable they want to observe. Hovering your mouse over individual circle on the scatterplot will also give you extra information regarding each observation.
